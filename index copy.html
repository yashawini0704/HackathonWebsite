AI Innovation Hackathon

Objective

To help all Crewmates explore and learn how to use AI tools (without coding) to solve real workplace problems. These solutions should help reduce manual work, save time, and make daily operations smoother across teams.

This will also help participants build confidence in using modern tools, stay relevant in their roles, and create real, usable & workable solutions to benefit teams across centres at DesiCrew.

Participation

  • Teams of 5 DesiCrew employees
  • Each team to be captained by 1 DNext leader

Judging Criteria

  • The actual problem
  • Ease of use by other teams
  • Effort and learning demonstrated

Post Hackathon

  • Top projects will be refined and rolled out with support from Vikram team
  • All working solutions will be added to an internal "AI Solutions Library"
